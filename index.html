What the Silverado offers

Built from high-strength, roll-formed steel to offer the legendary durability you expect from a Chevy pickup truck, the next generation offers a larger, more functional and more capable bed. New features include:

- Best-in-class cargo box volume so you can haul more with each load†
- Available 120v outlet and LED Rear Task Lighting make work easier wherever you go
- Three fixed tie-downs welded into each corner of the bed to help keep your cargo secure — rated at up to 500 lbs. each
- A CornerStep bumper with larger footwells to make getting into and out of the bed easier in work boots
